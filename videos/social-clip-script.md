# 📱 Social clip — shot list & caption script

A ready-to-film script for a short WebDev Academy promo. Built for **TikTok /
Reels / FB Stories**: **vertical 9:16**, **~30 seconds** (a 15s cutdown is at the
bottom). Record your **phone screen** on the live site, then assemble the clips
in **CapCut** with the captions and music below.

- **Site to film:** https://myominthet99.github.io/webdev-academy/
- **Screens used:** Home → Courses → a lesson → Daily Review → Certificate → CTA
- **Vibe:** fast, friendly, "you can actually build this." Myanmar teen audience.
- **Captions:** English on top line, Burmese (မြန်မာ) below. Big, high-contrast,
  safe-zone (keep text in the middle 80% — TikTok UI covers the edges).

> Tip: before filming, log in and finish one quick lesson so your streak,
> XP and a certificate are real on screen. Turn the phone to **Do Not Disturb**.

## 🎵 CapCut assembly notes

- **Music:** pick a trending upbeat track in CapCut (Myanmar or global). Cut on
  the beat — one screen change per beat keeps it snappy.
- **Transitions:** hard cuts or a quick "zoom in" between scenes. Avoid slow
  fades — they kill retention.
- **Text:** CapCut → Text → use the **bold** preset, white with a dark outline
  or a semi-transparent pill behind it. Add a tiny **pop-in** animation.
- **Speed:** speed the scrolling shots (2–3, 6) up to ~1.5×; keep the "payoff"
  shots (5 Daily Review, 7 Certificate) at 1× so they read.
- **Hook rule:** the first 1.5s must show motion + the promise. Don't open on a
  static logo — open on the animated code hero (scene 1).
- **Captions on:** ~60% of viewers watch muted, so the on-screen captions carry
  the message; the voiceover is a bonus.
- **Aspect:** project settings → **9:16**, 1080×1920. Export 1080p.
